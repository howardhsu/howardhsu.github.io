    Short Bio

    I am a Ph.D student in computer science at University of Illinois at Chicago, advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu and Prof. Bing Liu. Previously I got my master from Peking University.

    During my Ph.D. study I also work as a research intern at Facebook AI, Amazon AI and WeChat AI lab.

    Google Scholar, GitHub, Linkedin, Twitter, Medium

    Research Interest

    Task Representation Learning, Lifelong learning/Open-world Learning and Sentiment Analysis.
